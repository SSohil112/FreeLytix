About This Application

This interactive dashboard provides comprehensive analysis of Fiverr marketplace data, including earnings, ratings, and categories. Our goal is to help freelancers and businesses make data-driven decisions to optimize their Fiverr presence.

The application visualizes key metrics and trends from the Fiverr dataset, allowing users to explore different aspects of the marketplace and gain valuable insights.
